# Scene-Specific Optimization Guide

Detailed patterns for each scene type, extracted from 1,186 viral prompts.

## Product Photography

**Recommended terms:**
- Hasselblad quality
- Apple product aesthetics
- Premium advertising style
- Editorial commercial photography

**Recommended parameters:**
- Studio lighting setup
- High dynamic range
- 100mm macro lens, f/8 sharp focus
- White seamless background OR contextual lifestyle setting
- Floating/levitating composition for premium feel

**Common constraints:**
- No product distortion or warping
- No text watermarks
- Maintain accurate product proportions
- No busy backgrounds

**Template:**
```
[Product name], premium advertising photography, [background type].

Visual Style:
Hasselblad medium format quality, Apple-style product aesthetics. Clean, minimal, premium.

Lighting:
[High-key studio / soft natural / dramatic rim light], [shadow style].

Technical:
100mm lens, f/8, high dynamic range, 8K resolution.

Constraints:
Product must not be distorted. No text or watermarks. Maintain accurate proportions and details.
```

## Portrait Photography

**Recommended terms:**
- Wong Kar-wai aesthetics
- Annie Leibovitz style
- Saul Leiter through-window effect
- Christopher Doyle cinematography

**Recommended parameters:**
- 90mm lens, f/1.8
- Shallow depth of field
- Soft side lighting or neon reflections
- Kodak Vision3 500T film grain

**Common constraints:**
- Maintain realistic facial features
- Preserve identity characteristics
- No face obscuring (unless artistic intent)
- No uncanny valley effects

**Template:**
```
[Subject description], [mood/setting].

Visual Style:
[Artist reference] aesthetics. [Color palette]. Shot with 90mm lens, f/1.8, [film stock] grain.

Lighting & Atmosphere:
[Light source and quality]. [Environmental mood]. [Emotional undertone].

Constraints:
Maintain realistic facial features. Do not alter identity characteristics.
```

## Food Photography

**Recommended terms:**
- High-end culinary magazine style
- Bon Appétit editorial
- Michelin-star plating
- Gourmet food styling

**Recommended parameters:**
- 45-degree overhead angle OR eye-level hero shot
- 85mm lens, f/2.8
- Soft side backlight with fill
- Warm color temperature (3200K-4000K)

**Common constraints:**
- No utensil distractions
- No text overlays
- Maintain appetizing color temperature
- No artificial plastic textures

**Sensory additions (critical for food):**
- Steam rising, curling
- Glistening surfaces
- Caramelized edges
- "Aroma penetrates the frame"
- "Texture feels tangible"

**Template:**
```
[Dish name and description], [plating style].

Visual Style:
High-end culinary magazine aesthetic, [color palette]. Hasselblad medium format quality.

Composition:
[Angle] angle, 85mm lens, f/2.8, shallow depth of field on [focus point].

Sensory Details:
[Steam/motion], [textures], [temperature cues], [aroma suggestion].

Constraints:
No utensils in frame. No text. Maintain appetizing warm color temperature.
```

## Japanese Aesthetic

**Recommended terms:**
- Wabi-sabi aesthetics
- MUJI visual language
- Japanese airy feel (空気感)
- Ma (negative space)
- Mono no aware (pathos of things)

**Recommended parameters:**
- High-key photography
- Diffused natural light
- Soft shadows or shadowless
- Muted, desaturated palette
- Generous negative space

**Common constraints:**
- No high-saturation neon colors
- No artificial plastic textures
- No cluttered compositions
- Avoid harsh direct lighting

**Template:**
```
[Subject], Japanese aesthetic.

Visual Style:
Wabi-sabi aesthetics, [specific element]. MUJI visual language. Muted [color palette].

Lighting:
High-key, diffused natural light. Soft shadows. [Time of day].

Composition:
Generous negative space (Ma). [Simple geometric arrangement]. Clean minimalism.

Atmosphere:
[Seasonal reference]. Quiet contemplation. [Sensory whisper].

Constraints:
No high-saturation colors. No cluttered elements. Maintain serene, breathing composition.
```

Materials:
[Surface properties]. [Subsurface scattering if applicable]. [Reflectivity].

[Solid color / gradient / contextual].
